Antimicrobial Stewardship Publications

Our mission is to enhance dairy health by conducting cutting-edge research and providing actionable insights that help improve the well-being of dairy animals and the quality of dairy products. As a team of dedicated scientists, veterinarians, and researchers, we are committed to fostering a healthier and more sustainable dairy industry.
