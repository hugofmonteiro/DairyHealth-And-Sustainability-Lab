Microbial Genomics and Nutrition Publications

Our research is centered on understanding how diet impacts the health, performance, and reproductive success of dairy cattle. We explore the relationships between nutrient intake, metabolism, and milk production, with a focus on improving feed efficiency and optimizing dietary formulations for different stages of lactation and growth. By analyzing the effects of various feed ingredients, additives, and feeding strategies, we aim to enhance cattle nutrition for improved milk yield, better fertility, and overall health. Our research also examines how specific nutrients, such as energy, protein, and minerals, affect metabolic pathways and their role in preventing common diseases like ketosis and mastitis.
