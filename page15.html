Infectious Diseases Publications

Our research team is committed to advancing the understanding and management of infectious diseases in dairy cattle through innovative studies and cutting-edge technologies. We focus on improving disease prevention, early detection, and treatment strategies to enhance herd health and productivity. Our research spans critical areas such as pathogen identification, immune response mechanisms, biosecurity practices, and the development of vaccines and antimicrobial alternatives. By addressing the challenges posed by infectious diseases, we aim to optimize dairy cattle health, increase milk production efficiency, and support the sustainability of dairy farming practices.
