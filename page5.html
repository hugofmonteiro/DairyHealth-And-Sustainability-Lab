About Us

01. Our Mission

At the heart of our research program is a commitment to generating groundbreaking knowledge, innovative tools, and effective strategies. Our mission is to propel advancements in fertility, health, and performance, specifically tailored to enhance the resilience, well-being, longevity, and productivity of dairy cows.​

02. Our Vision

We are passionately committed to cultivating a thriving and sustainable dairy community. Our mission is threefold: to champion the dairy industry's sustainability, fortify food supply security, and lead the way in environmental stewardship by improving dairy cows' health, fertility, and production efficiency. Through innovation, collaboration, and a deep-rooted dedication to our values, we aspire to create a resilient and responsible dairy sector that cares for our dairy cows, nourishes communities and preserves our planet for future generations​

03. Our Approach

In pursuing sustainability, we conduct extensive research to uncover innovative solutions to promote health, fertility, and production efficiency in dairy cows. Through this exploration, we've discovered that optimizing efficiency and minimizing resource consumption is a highly successful strategy to promote a resilient dairy sector. Our approach revolves around pioneering research that enhances reproductive performance, health, and key performance traits—such as milk yield, components, and feed efficiency. We aim to significantly minimize the dairy carbon footprint by focusing on these aspects, fostering a more sustainable and resilient dairy community.​
